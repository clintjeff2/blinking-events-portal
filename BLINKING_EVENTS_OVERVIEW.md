## Blinking Events Application Overview

The **Blinking Events Mobile Application** is designed to provide clients with a seamless, mobile-first platform to discover services, request quotes, and manage their event planning experience directly from their phones.

- The **mobile app** emphasizes personalization, interactivity, and client-event management tools, ensuring a premium and engaging user journey.
- The **website** continues to market the brand broadly.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Service Catalog & Discovery

**Description:**  
Central hub for browsing Blinking Events’ service offerings.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Categorized service listings (e.g., Wedding, Corporate, Cultural, etc.)
- Search & filter by service type, location, or event type
- Transparent price ranges and package previews

---

### 2. Service Provider Profiles

**Description:**  
Personnel-focused service discovery.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Staff profiles (hostesses, MCs, security, etc.)
- Skills & qualifications with media
- Language proficiencies
- Availability calendar for booking dates
- Portfolio of past events worked

---

### 3. Order Request System

**Description:**  
Custom event request and quotation process.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Select multiple services for a single event
- Date & time selection
- Venue details (location, guest count)
- Add special requirements/notes
- Budget range input
- Quote request submission
- Order status tracking (e.g., Pending, Quoted, Confirmed)

---

### 4. Client Portal

**Description:**  
Personal workspace for clients.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Secure user registration/login
- Order history (past and current requests)
- Favorites (save preferred services/staff)
- Notifications (real-time updates on quotes & bookings)
- Direct messaging with the Blinking Events team
- Document upload (event briefs, reference files)
- Event timeline tracking with milestones

---

### 5. Media Gallery

**Description:**  
Visual showcase of Blinking Events’ portfolio.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Browse past events by category
- Before/after transformation views
- High-quality videos & highlight reels
- Virtual tours with 360° venue/decor setups

---

### 6. Contact & Support

**Description:**  
Direct communication and help.

**Key Functionalities:**
- One-click contact (call, WhatsApp, email)
- FAQs & knowledge base
- Live chat for instant support
- Emergency contact (for urgent clients with bookings)

---

## Admin Features

### 1. Service Management

**Description:**  
Tools to manage and update app content.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Add/edit services with details & pricing
- Manage staff profiles with bios & photos
- Upload & categorize images
- Update service packages and tiers

---

### 2. Order Management

**Description:**  
Manage incoming client requests.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Quotation builder & send feature
- Update order status (Pending, Quoted, Confirmed, Completed)
- Direct client communication in-app

---

### 3. Analytics Dashboard

**Description:**  
Insights into service demand & business performance.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Track most requested services
- Monitor seasonal demand trends
- Revenue & bookings reports
- Analyze client demographics & preferences
- Engagement metrics (push notification performance, app activity)

---

### 4. Marketing Tools

**Description:**  
Promote and engage with clients.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Publish special offers & discounts
- Push notifications (service updates, promotions)
- Feature selected services or staff
- Manage and publish client testimonials

---

### 5. Web Portal

**Description:**  
Desktop access for staff/admins.

**Key Functionalities:**
- Admin dashboard for secure login
- Centralized control over app content
- Role-based permissions (limit access by staff role)
- Reporting tools with export/download options
